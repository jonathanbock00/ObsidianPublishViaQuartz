# Ingredients
#buy
- [ ] 500 ml Water
- [ ] 4 bananas
- [ ] 10 pitted dates
- [ ] 70 g sweet lupin flour
- [ ] 20 g hemp seeds
- [ ] date syrup or agave nectar
- [ ] spirulina powder
# Directions
1. Mix all ingredients except the sweet lupin flour for 30 seconds in a high powered blender.
2. Mix again for 30 seconds.
