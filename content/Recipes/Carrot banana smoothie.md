# Ingredients
#buy
- [x] 2 frozen bananas
- [x] 1 cup carrots - peeled and roughly chopped
- [x] 1 tbsp lemon juice
- [x] 1 cup oat milk
- [x] 1 tbsp hemp seeds
- [x] ½ tsp cinnamon
- [x] ⅛ tsp nutmeg
# Directions
- Blend all ingredients in a high power blender for 40 seconds.
